    Welcome to TangZheng’s Homepage!!

    I am a junior majoring in Data Science and Big Data Technology at Yangtze University and have been fortunate to be mentored by Prof. RuiDeng and Prof. Guozhong Gao. For more information, please check my CV.

    In the well signal data detected by optical fiber vibration signal, I proposed to distinguish the oil and water layers of production wells based on mean clustering, solve the distribution of wellbore flow, and comprehensively determine the best perforated formation, which greatly avoided the damage to the geological environment caused by drilling at the depth of the formation and saved the economic losses caused by multiple drilling.
